</li><li>Accessible, Yes 
</li><li>Number of Levels, 3

<h3>Designated Waiting Areas (DWAs)</h3>\r
<img alt="TTC Customers at Designated Waiting Area on subway platform."  border="0" hspace="0" src="../../../images/Features/DWA.jpg" vspace="0px"/>\r
<p>DWAs are located on all subway platforms for your safety and convenience while you are waiting for your train.</p>
<p>A DWA has brighter lights as well as an intercom with access to the collector, a closed-circuit television camera, a public telephone, and a bench to sit on while you wait.</p>
